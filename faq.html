Good to know

Frequently asked questions

The things hosts ask us most. Don’t see yours? We’re happy to answer — just reach out.

Do you handle same-day, back-to-back turnovers?

Yes — tight checkout-to-check-in windows are exactly what we’re built for. We schedule from your booking calendar so we’re there right after checkout, and optional par-stock linen sets make same-day turns even faster.

Do you provide linens and restock supplies?

We do. We can launder your linens on-site or swap in fresh hotel-grade sets, and we track and replenish guest consumables — toiletries, paper goods, coffee, and more — so your listing is always fully stocked.

How do I know the clean was done right?

After every turnover you receive a completed checklist and photos of the guest-ready home. If we spot damage or a maintenance issue, we flag it immediately with photos so you can act before your next guest arrives.

Can you sync with Airbnb, VRBO, or Turno?

Yes. We schedule turnovers directly from your booking calendar and work seamlessly with Turno and similar platforms, so cleans are dispatched automatically at each checkout — no manual scheduling on your end.

Are you insured and bonded?

Yes — we’re fully insured and bonded, so your property and belongings are protected. Ask us for our certificate of insurance and we’ll send it before your first turn. As fellow STR owners, we hold ourselves to the standard we’d want in our own home.

What areas do you serve?

The greater Pikes Peak region — Colorado Springs, Manitou Springs, Woodland Park, Monument, Black Forest, Falcon, Peyton, Fountain, Cascade, and Green Mountain Falls. Not sure if we reach you? Just ask.

How does pricing work?

We charge a flat per-turnover rate based on your property’s size and needs — starting at $109 for a studio/1BR. Linen service, restocking, and deep cleans are add-ons, and hosts with 3+ properties get portfolio pricing. We confirm your exact rate after a quick walkthrough, with no hidden fees. See full pricing.

Are you a property management company?

No — and we never will be. We’re a cleaning specialist and your eyes on the ground, nothing more. We’ll never compete for your listing or pitch to manage it. Our only job is making you look great to your guests.

Still have questions?

We’re real people — just ask.

Call, text, or send a message and we’ll get back to you the same day with straight answers and a clear quote.
